Frequently Asked Questions - FAQ

I am unable to order, despite the store being close by

Please share your location with us through our online platform or WhatsApp number (8291082910), and we'll show you the closest store that can deliver to you. You can also contact our customer service team for further assistance.

How do I search for particular product?

You may look for a product by browsing the category pages or using the search bar at the top of each page.

Can I send the photo of the order?

Yes you can order by sending a photo of your list on the chat, please ensure that the photo is clear and handwriting is readable

Can I send voice note of the order?

Currently, we do not accept orders via Voice-Notes. You can try our catalogue-based ordering system by clicking on the link below -shop.moreretail.in You can also send us a list of items you want to order via chat through our national delivery number 8291082910

Can I type my order in local language?

Yes. You have the option of typing your order in your local language

How are the fruits and vegetables packaged?

Fruits and vegetables are hand picked and hand cleaned. Freshness and quality check is done and we ensure careful handling of all our products in a hygienic condition.

How will you ensure the freshness of products?

All the products are maintained at correct temperatures with suitable packaging. They would be handled carefully and in a hygienic manner.

How are the fruits and vegetables weighed?

Fruits and vegetables are weighed on an approved scale. The actual weight can vary a little than order details due to the inherent characteristic of fruits and vegetables. Any difference of +/- 5% will be adjusted accordingly during billing. The remaining balance / refunds will be settled at the time of delivery.

Do you deliver Ice cream, Milk, Curd, Frozen(McCain)?

Yes. We have a wide variety of SKUs available for delivery of frozen and dairy items. Please browse through our web-based catalogue and select "Dairy & Frozen food" category. You can also use the search bar for finding the item of your choice.
